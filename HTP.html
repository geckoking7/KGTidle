Controls

AWSD - Move

Arrow keys - Shoot

E - interact

Rewards / purpose of existing

The better you do, the more rewards you collect (XP, gold (basically xp), and some other goofy things)

The xp reward is scaled in some boring quardratic line (was interesting but now bad)

Gameplay

All days will have a little shadow on the far right, that follows you and instantly kills you if you touch him, however, it is very slow

Each day he becomes faster and faster, making him more of a threat

There are also zones that all have different features and things to kill you with there own respective objectives

That line was meant to get your hopes up, the time till a second zone is made will be in forever

Outside

Grass

Outside is the first zone, having cars that can run over you, and thats it

Objectives

Catch the desert eagle

Shoot down the desert eagle with the desert eagle

Find and kill the imposter

Walk around the people, and if an E shows, that means they're the imposter

The imposter will kill someone, at random, every few seconds

But, if all innocents die and the imposter is not found, you will be the next kill

If you do find the imposter, kill him, and if you can't, just jump infront of a car
